Biobank Information Management System (BIMS) for a Genetic Research Institution

The Biobank Information Management System (BIMS) is designed to optimize the management of biospecimens in a genetic research institution. BIMS allows biobank managers to efficiently oversee sample organization and inventory management, while providing researchers with a streamlined process for requesting and accessing samples. This integrated approach enhances workflow efficiency and supports cutting-edge research.
