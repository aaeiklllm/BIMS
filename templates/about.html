Biobank Information Management System (BIMS)

Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What is the Biobank Information Management System (BIMS)?
A: BIMS is a web-based platform designed to streamline the management and tracking of biobanked samples. It allows biobank managers to easily track and manage samples, while offering researchers a streamlined process for requesting and accessing samples.

Q: How do I create an account on BIMS?
A: To create an account, visit the BIMS homepage and choose 'Login as Biobank Manager' or 'Login as Researcher.' Follow the on-screen instructions to register. You'll need to provide necessary information and verify your email address.

Q: What should I do if I forget my password?
A: Click 'Forgot Password?' on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email. For additional help, contact our support team at techassist.bims@gmail.com.

Q: How can I request a sample?
A: Log in to your researcher account, navigate to the 'Request Sample' section, and complete the request form. You will receive confirmation and tracking information once your request is processed.

Q: Can I track the status of my sample request?
A: Yes, after submitting a request, you can track its status through the 'My Requests' section in your account dashboard. You will also receive email notifications regarding updates.

Q: How does BIMS handle data security and privacy?
A: Access to sensitive information is restricted based on user roles to ensure privacy. BIMS complies with standard data protection regulations.

Q: Who can I contact for technical support?
A: For technical assistance, please reach out to our support team at techassist.bims@gmail.com. We are dedicated to promptly resolving any issues you encounter.

Q: Can I update my account information?
A: Yes, you can update your account information by logging in and accessing the 'My Profile' section. Make sure to save any changes you make.

Q: What do I do if I encounter a problem with the system?
A: If you experience any issues with the system, please report them to our support team with a detailed description of the problem. We will work to resolve it as quickly as possible.

Q: How can I provide feedback about BIMS?
A: We welcome feedback from users to help improve our system. Please send your comments and suggestions to techassist.bims@gmail.com.
